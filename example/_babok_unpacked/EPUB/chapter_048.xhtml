.1 Decision Making

Decisions are made throughout the initiative. A stakeholder may serve in various roles in the decision-making process such as: • participant in decision-making discussions, • subject matter expert (SME) lending experience and knowledge to the decision-making process, • reviewer of information, and • approver of decisions.

The decision-making process defines what happens when teams cannot reach consensus, by identifying escalation paths and key stakeholders who hold final decision-making authority.
