Elements

.1 Decomposition Objectives

Objectives of functional decomposition both drive the process of decomposition and define what to decompose, how to decompose, and how deeply to decompose.

The objectives may include: contribute to the overall result, or to identify important metrics and indicators. object of design. or phenomenon in isolation from its encompassing environment. breaking down a complex value into its constituent factors. function for various processes. improve process quality. or a function easily replaceable without impacting the system as a whole.
